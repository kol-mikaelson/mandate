We publish from Chennai because we believe this city — and this state — deserves analysis commensurate with its complexity.

Tamil Nadu is not an afterthought in the national conversation. It is a laboratory of governance, a cradle of culture, and a place where the contradictions of modern India play out with particular intensity. Yet its political and civic life remains chronically under-examined by national media, distorted by stereotypes, or reduced to electoral arithmetic.

**The Madras Mandate exists to change this.**

## What We Cover

We are concerned primarily with three domains: **State Policy**, the machinery of government and legislation; **Urban Governance**, the lived experience of city administration; and the **Cultural Mandate**, the arts, cinema, and institutions that shape Tamil life.

## Who We Are

We are journalists, academics, lawyers, and citizens who believe that serious analysis serves a democratic function. We have no interest in neutrality as an alibi for the absence of rigour.

This is the mandate.
